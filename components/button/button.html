Buttons

arrow_forward_ios

Buttons allow users to take actions, and make choices, with a single tap.

Types of Button

There are total 3 types of button components are availabl btn-contained , btn-text , btn-outline .

Contained Button

The Contained button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .

Outline Button

The Outlined button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .

Text Button

The Text button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .

Icon Button

The Icon button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .
Also there icons can be placed at beginning , end or only icon.

Link Button

The Link button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .

Floating Action Button

The Floating Action button components are available in five variant red, yellow , orange , teal , purple .
